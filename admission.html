Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure admission

1
Fill Application Form

Complete the online application form with all required details. Ensure all information is accurate and up-to-date.

2
Submit Documents

Upload or submit all required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and identification proofs.

3
Entrance Examination

Attend the scheduled entrance examination. The test evaluates basic knowledge and aptitude of the student.

4
Interview Process

Participate in the interview with the student and parents. This helps us understand the student's personality and goals.

5
Complete Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment process by paying fees and submitting final documents.

School Rules & Regulations

Attendance: Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory for all students.
Uniform: School uniform must be worn on all school days and during school functions.
Punctuality: Students must arrive on time. Late arrivals may face disciplinary action.
Discipline: Respectful behavior towards teachers, staff, and fellow students is expected at all times.
Mobile Phones: Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited during school hours.
Examinations: All students must appear for all examinations. No retest will be conducted.
Leave Application: Prior permission is required for any leave. Medical certificates are mandatory for sick leave.
Fee Payment: School fees must be paid on time. Late payment may result in additional charges.
Parent-Teacher Meetings: Regular attendance at PTM is expected for better student progress.
Library Rules: Books must be returned on time. Damaged or lost books must be replaced.
